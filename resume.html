PAPER PUBLICATION

Title: Two level Authentication for E- Voting system using IOT technology.(.tjarset)

Two-level authentication for an e-voting system using IoT technology enhances security by requiring voters to verify their identity through two distinct methods. Typically, this involves a combination of something the voter knows (e.g. a password or PIN) and something the voter has (e.g. a smartphone or biometric device). This dual layered approach significantly reduces the risk of fraud and unauthorized access, ensuring a more secure and trustworthy e-voting process.
